How to Use

  • Select Date: Pick a date directly from the calendar or use the date picker above (defaults to today).

  • View Progress: The chart shows your last 7 days. Calendar highlights logged dates in your active theme color.
  • Stay Consistent: Track daily to maintain streaks, unlock badges, and hit weekly goals.
