FAQ
How do I log study time?
Go to Time Tracker, enter hours for today, and click Save. Your data appears in charts and calendar.
Can I use Learny offline?
Yes! Learny is a PWA. After first load, you can use it without internet.
Is my data safe?
All data is stored locally on your device using localStorage. No servers involved.
How does Pomodoro work?
Set a duration (default 25 min), start the timer, and focus. When it ends, take a break.
How to earn badges?
Log study hours weekly. Reach 60% for Bronze, 100% for Silver, 130% for Golden.
Can I change the theme?
Yes! Go to Settings and choose Purple, Red, Green, Blue, or Yellow.
How to add music?
In Music page, click Upload and select MP3 files from your device.
